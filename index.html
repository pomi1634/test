In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful. Uthman Kheel Public School is situated in Bajaur Agency, about 3km from agency headquater. To the north of school is salarzai, to the south is famous mountain kohi nor, to the east is shamozai and to the west is khar bazaar. It was set up for a sacred purpose of knowledge in 28th may 2001.As it was felt that the people of the arias of alizai and barters were very backward in the field of education as compare to other tribes of Bajaur Agency. Everyone knows that in Uthman Kheel tribes considered spending of money on education as the less of wealth. The people of this area were trapped by ignorance, darkness, poverty and enemies. NO one was ready to admit his child in a standard school on fees.
